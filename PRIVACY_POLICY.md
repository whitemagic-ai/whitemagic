## 1. Introduction

WhiteMagic ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Local-First (Default Mode)
WhiteMagic is **local-first by default**. In local mode:
- **All data stays on your machine** in `~/.whitemagic/`
- **No account required** â€” no email, no sign-up
- **No telemetry** is sent to any server
- **You own your data** completely

### 2.2 Cloud-Optional Mode
If you choose to use optional cloud features (sync, hosted API):
- **Email address**: For account creation
- **Subscription details**: Plan tier, payment status

### 2.2 API Usage Data
- **API keys**: Securely hashed, used for authentication
- **Request logs**: Timestamps, endpoints, response codes
- **Usage metrics**: Request counts, storage used, memory counts

### 2.3 Content You Store
- **Memories**: Text content you store via API
- **Tags and metadata**: Associated with your memories
- **Timestamps**: Creation and modification dates

### 2.4 Technical Information
- **IP addresses**: For security and rate limiting
- **User agent**: Browser/client information
- **Request IDs**: For debugging and support

## 4. Data Sharing

### 4.1 We Do NOT
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your memories to train AI models
- Share your content with advertisers
- Provide your data to data brokers

### 4.2 We May Share With
- **Whop**: For payment processing only
- **Infrastructure providers**: AWS, hosting services (data encrypted)
- **Law enforcement**: If legally required
- **Service providers**: Under strict confidentiality agreements

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Security Measures
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
- **API keys**: Hashed with SHA-256 before storage
- **Access control**: Strict user isolation
- **Regular audits**: Security reviews and updates

### 5.2 Your Responsibilities
- Keep API keys secure
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Rotate keys regularly
- Report security issues immediately

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Active Accounts
- Memories: Retained indefinitely
- Logs: 90 days
- Usage metrics: 24 months

### 6.2 Cancelled Accounts
- Data preserved for 30 days
- Then permanently deleted
- Backups may retain data up to 90 days

### 6.3 Free Tier Inactivity
- 180 days of inactivity triggers deletion warning
- 30 days to reactivate
- Then permanent deletion
